CCTV Installation & Smart Surveillance

Safeguard your business with modern, intelligent, and IoT-integrated surveillance systems.

NovaSphere IT Solutions provides professional CCTV installation and smart surveillance solutions designed to protect businesses, institutions, and homes. We specialize in IP-based camera systems, remote viewing, and AI-powered video analytics.

Whether you need an indoor or outdoor security system, our experts ensure end-to-end deployment — from design and installation to network integration and real-time monitoring.

Why Choose Our CCTV & Surveillance Services

We combine modern hardware and IoT-based analytics to give you complete visibility and peace of mind.

High-Definition Surveillance

Crystal-clear HD and 4K camera systems for indoor and outdoor monitoring with night vision capabilities.

Smart Network Integration

Connect your CCTV systems to secure networks for remote access and real-time monitoring via mobile or web apps.

AI Video Analytics

Detect motion, recognize faces, and analyze activities with intelligent AI and ML algorithms.

Cloud Storage & Management
